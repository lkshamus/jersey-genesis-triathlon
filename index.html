High School Challenge and Bambino Adventure Race

High School Challenge

Three students from the same high school may enter as a team in the Triathlon. Teams consist of one swimmer, one runner and one biker (all males, all females or co-eds). The three teams with the best finishing times will receive awards. Ocean First Bank has provided additional sponsorship for a traveling school trophy for the team with the best finishing time. The trophy will be kept by the winning school and returned the following year to the race director to be presented to the new winning team.

Bambino Adventure Race

New for 2019! This one-mile traffic closed course will have several obstacles to be conquered before completing the race. Oh yeah kids, come ready to get dirty! This is not a USAT Event, no additional insurance fee is required.
